Purpose and Objectives

The purpose of this course is to explain how Section 508 requirements apply to ICT micro-purchases. To achieve this goal, we will follow Mary through the process of acquiring her multifunction machine.

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Define Section 508 and how it applies to your procurement
  • Identify a micro-purchase
  • Determine the impact of Section 508 standards on micro-purchases
