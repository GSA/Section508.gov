Accessibility for All

Section 508 addresses accessibility for people with disabilities, including but not limited to disabilities that affect:

  • Vision
  • Hearing
  • Perception of color
  • Speech
  • Manipulation, reach, and strength
  • Language and cognitive abilities

Section 508 applies to all ICT products and services that you purchase, including small dollar purchases known as micro-purchases.

It is recommended that you review the standards and regulations listed below to further your understanding about Section 508 and how you can support implementation.

  • Section 508.gov
  • Section 508 Standards
  • Section 508 Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)
  • Micropurchase and the FAR
