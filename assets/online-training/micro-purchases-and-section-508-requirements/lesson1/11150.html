Agency Specific Policies and Procedures

While all agencies are required to adhere to Section 508, procedures may differ from one agency to another.

As a cardholder, it is your responsibility to be aware of any agency-specific policies and procedures related to ICT micro-purchases and Section 508. If you are unsure of any agency-specific policies and procedures, check with your agency's Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO), Procurement Officer, and Section 508 Program Manager.
