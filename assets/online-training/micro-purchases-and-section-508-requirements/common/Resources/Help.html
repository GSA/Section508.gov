Help

The current Screen Title displays at the top of each screen in the course.

The Current Topic displays beneath the Course title on each screen of the course.

The Resources link opens a new window containing a list of job aids additional course materials, and links to related information. You may read, download, or print any of these documents at any time.

The Glossary link opens a list of frequently used terms related to Section 508.

The Help link opens a window that explains the layout of the courseware and its functionality.

The Exit link allows you to exit the course at any time, bookmarking the current screen for when you return.

User Instructions display on screen where they are needed.

Within any lesson, you may navigate forward and backward one screen using the Next and Back buttons at the bottom of the screen.

The screen indicator, located between these two buttons, identifies where the current screen falls in relationship to the total number of screens in the lesson.
