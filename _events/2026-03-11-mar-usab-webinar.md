Now in its third year, the Governmentwide Section 508 Assessment evaluates how well federal agencies provide accessible and usable information and communication technology (ICT) consistent with Section 508 requirements. The presenters will provide an overview of the FY 2025 Governmentwide Section 508 Assessment, including key findings and recommendations for Congress and federal agencies.

### Speakers:
* **Andrew Nielson**, Director of the Government-wide IT Accessibility Program, Office of Government-wide Policy , General Services Administration
* **Kristen Smith-O'Connor**, Senior ICT Accessibility Specialist, Office of Government-wide IT Accessibility Program, Office of Government-wide Policy, General Services Administration
* **Alex Wilson**, Program Analyst , Office of Government-wide Policy, General Services Administration
