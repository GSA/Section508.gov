## Course Details
**Audience:** 
* Document designers / content creators
* Section 508 Program Managers
* Section 508 Subject Matter Experts (SME)

**Duration:** 85 minutes  
**Difficulty Level:** Beginner  
**Version:** 1.0 (Apr 2026)  

## What Will I Learn in This Course?
This training is the first of two courses that will enable you to create accessible text documents.

After completing this training, you will be able to do the following:
<ol class="usa-icon-list tablet:grid-col">
  <li class="usa-icon-list__item">
      <div class="usa-icon-list__icon text-green"><svg class="usa-icon" aria-hidden="true" role="img"><use xlink:href="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sprite.svg#check_circle"></use></svg></div>
      <div class="usa-icon-list__content">Organize your text document structure using headings and lists.</div>
  </li>
  <li class="usa-icon-list__item">
      <div class="usa-icon-list__icon text-green"><svg class="usa-icon" aria-hidden="true" role="img"><use xlink:href="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sprite.svg#check_circle"></use></svg></div>
      <div class="usa-icon-list__content">Use color appropriately in text documents.</div>
  </li>
  <li class="usa-icon-list__item">
      <div class="usa-icon-list__icon text-green"><svg class="usa-icon" aria-hidden="true" role="img"><use xlink:href="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sprite.svg#check_circle"></use></svg></div>
      <div class="usa-icon-list__content">Add accessible information to the document, such as language labels and file names.</div>
  </li>
  <li class="usa-icon-list__item">
      <div class="usa-icon-list__icon text-green"><svg class="usa-icon" aria-hidden="true" role="img"><use xlink:href="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sprite.svg#check_circle"></use></svg></div>
      <div class="usa-icon-list__content">Test your document for accessibility and share it as a text document or as a PDF file.</div>
  </li>
</ol>

**In the second course in this series**, you will learn how to use specific features accessibly. This will allow you to add images, tables, columns, and other features in a way that will allow all users to access and understand the information in the document.
