## Course Details
**Audience:** Chief Acquisition Officers, Chief Information Officers, and Section 508 Program Manager
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Skill Level:** Beginner | Intermediate | Expert  
**Version:** Version 1.0

## What will I learn in this course?
This course is designed to provide Federal government Chief Information Officer (CIO) and Chief Acquisition Officer (CAO) offices, and Agency Section 508 Program Managers guidance on what is needed to establish a methodical approach to creating and implementing a Technology Accessibility Program.
