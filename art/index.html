What's new in DART 2.1

DART 2.1 includes important updates that will improve the management of Section 508 requirements in DHS solicitations:

Key changes:

  • Solicitation Language Changes
    • Added Section 508 Deliverables Section to recommended solicitation language
    • Deleted Instructions to Offerors and Acceptance Criteria Sections
    • Revised requirements related to contractor validation for 508 conformance
    • Added requirements for cloud services, kiosks, and hardware
    • More information: Guide to Understanding the DART 2.1 Section 508 Contract Language Output

  • DART 2.1 Functionality Changes
    • Improved instructions to guide users through the entire solicitation development process
    • Added ability to export all outputs to Microsoft Word
    • Added new Project Planning Section to assist with obtain a detailed list of applicable Section 508 standards at the beginning of a project
    • Added new Market Research Section to assist with addressing Section 508 in Requests for Information (RFIs)
