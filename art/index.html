What's new in ART

ART includes important functionality updates that improve efficiencies and create a better user experience with developing Section 508 requirements:

Key changes:

  • Updates to Solicitation Language
    • Added Section 508 Deliverables Section to recommended solicitation language
    • Deleted Instructions to Offerors and Acceptance Criteria Sections
    • Revised requirements related to contractor validation for 508 conformance
    • Added requirements for cloud services, kiosks, and hardware
    • Improved instructions to guide users through the entire solicitation development process
    • Added ability to export all outputs to Microsoft Word
    • New Project Planning Section to assist with obtaining a detailed list of applicable Section 508 standards at the beginning of a project
    • New Market Research Section to assist with addressing Section 508 in Requests for Information (RFIs)
